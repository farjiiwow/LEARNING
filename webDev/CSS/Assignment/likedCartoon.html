Cartoons I Like
Chhota Bheem

Chhota Bheem is an Indian animated series adventures about a boy named Bheem and his friends in fictional village of Dholakpur. Bheem and his friends are usually involved in protecting the village from various evil forces.

Pokemon

The Pokémon cartoon is about Ash Ketchum, a 10-year-old Pokémon trainer from Pallet Town, and his Pikachu, who are on a journey to become the world's greatest Pokémon Master. Ash makes many friends, including Misty, a water-type Pokémon trainer, and Brock, a Pokémon breeder.

Doraemon

Doraemon is a male cat robot from the 22nd century, with no ears and a blue color, travels back in time with the help of a time machine into the 21st century to help a boy who belongs to “The Nobi family” named Nobita Nobi, another main character of the series.

Ninja Hattori

Hattori a ninja boy who has come from the Iga mountains is now a part of the Mitsuba family along with his brother Shinzo and his ninja dog. Hattori helps Kenichi with his problems and constantly keeps an eye on him as a good friend.

Shinchan

Shinnosuke Nohara is 5 years old and goes to kindergarten. He loves beautiful ladies and stirs things up by always going at his own pace. This kindergartner certainly knows how to shake things up, but that's Shinchan, the most popular kid in Japan.
